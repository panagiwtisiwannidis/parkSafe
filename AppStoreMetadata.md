## Description (English — up to 4000 chars)

Never forget where you parked again.

ParkSafe saves your parking spot with a single tap — no account, no subscription, no tracking. Just open the app, tap Save, and find your car whenever you need it.

**Save your spot instantly**
Tap once to pin your exact GPS location. ParkSafe captures your coordinates and reverse-geocodes the address automatically so you always know the street name.

**Navigate back with ease**
Open turn-by-turn walking directions in Apple Maps or Google Maps directly from the app. No copy-pasting coordinates.

**Hourly reminders**
Enable optional notifications and ParkSafe will remind you every hour for up to 12 hours — so you never overstay a meter or forget where you left the car.

**100% private, 100% local**
Your location data never leaves your device. No servers, no analytics, no ads. Everything is stored locally using Apple's UserDefaults.

**Clean, distraction-free design**
Designed for one-handed use. Dark UI that's easy to read in sunlight or at night.
